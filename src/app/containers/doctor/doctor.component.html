Meet Our Doctors

At AIH, we have developed a team of highly qualified and experienced staff working with care and compassion. The team consists of doctors with proven international working experience and solid educational background from American and European institutions. Our heads of departments are leading Professors, Associate Professors and Doctors, each with more than 20 years of experience in the health sector.

When visiting our doctors, patients at AIH will always be provided with sufficient information on health conditions, treatment options and aftercare support. For our medical team, being a good doctor requires not only qualifications but also a deep empathy towards patients and families’ desires.

We understand that clinical research and training are of great value to the professional development of a hospital. For that reason, AIH has now established strategic alliances with world well-known medical systems in the U.S. The collaboration is intended to enhance existing services and to support the expansion of new medical services that would be offered at American International Hospital, by leveraging the expertise and resources in clinical excellence, education, and digital innovations.
