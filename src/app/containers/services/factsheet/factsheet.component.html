How we excel

At AIH, we make every endeavor to pursue our constant goal of being patient centric and excellent in services with the mission “to bring American standard healthcare services to patients and their families”. Coming to AIH, customers can easily access American healthcare services right here in Vietnam. For that reason, we attach great importance to investing in personnel, state-of-the-art facilities and equipment, as well as applying latest medical techniques in diagnosis and treatment from the US. We will also strongly and relentlessly continue to make continuous improvements in everything we do every day with the goal “To become one of the most trusted healthcare providers in South East Asia”.

The modern system of American "LDRP rooms"

AIH uses a system of American standard LDRP (Labor, Delivery, Recovery and Postpartum) rooms equipped with “4 in 1” facilities: Once a pregnant woman is placed in a room of this kind, this is the only room she and her family will use from labor, delivery (vaginal delivery) until stabilization. LDRP rooms help limit risks and pains for moms and babies, especially in cases of difficult birth.

Excellent care

Aiming at bringing patients the comfort they deserve, we have a dedicated team of patient service staff trained under American standards to give patients 24/7 support. 100% of inpatients are offered aftercare support. Our team can speak multi languages such as Vietnamese, English, French, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Cambodian.

Comprehensive application of American standards

At AIH, rooms and services comply to the new American standard model to provide the most safe and convenient experience to patients and families.

State-of-the-art equipment and facilities

AIH has invested in advanced medical equipment and facilities which are carefully selected and imported from world leading medical equipment providers e.g. Olympus (Japan), Karl Storz (Germany), Johnsons & Johnsons, GE Healthcare, Seiler (US), etc., ensuring the accuracy and efficiency in diagnosis and treatment.
